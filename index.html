Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how History Was Weird ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our TikTok or YouTube content publishing service.

1. Information We Collect

We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish video content to our own TikTok account. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from third-party users.

The only data accessed via the TikTok API is:

  • Our own account's basic profile information (used for authentication)
  • Video publishing status (used to confirm successful uploads)

2. How We Use Information

We use the TikTok API exclusively to:

  • Authenticate our own TikTok account
  • Upload video content to our own account
  • Check the publish status of uploaded videos

We do not use any data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. No user data is collected from viewers or followers.

4. Data Retention

We store only authentication tokens required to operate the TikTok API integration. These tokens are stored locally and are not shared with any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with:

  • TikTok – subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube – subject to Google's Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
