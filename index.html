Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how History Was Weird handles information when you use our TikTok or YouTube content publishing service.

We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish video content to our own TikTok account. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from third-party users.

2. How We Use Information

We use the TikTok API exclusively to authenticate our own account, upload video content, and check publish status. We do not use any data for advertising or analytics.

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. No user data is collected from viewers or followers.

4. Data Retention

We store only authentication tokens required to operate the TikTok API. These tokens are stored locally and are not shared with any third party.
